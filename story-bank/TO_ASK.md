# Questions to Ask (General) - for screening call 

## Top
- **ROLE**: How open-ended is the role? Is it mainly building new things or implementing existing solutions? 
	- What kinds of things are built
	- Based on my backgrond, are there other roles I should consider along with this one?  
- **TEAM**: Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with? Whats the size and typical background. 
- **PROBLEMS**: whats the biggest problem or bottleneck the team is trying to solve right now? 
- **GROWTH**: What does growth look like in this role? Where do people go from here in 5 or 10 years? 
- **PROCESS**: what's the timeline and what can i expect from the hiring process? 
	- Whats the format of the technical rounds? 
	- What skills or areas do they focus on? 
- **SUCCESS**: What does it take to be successful here? 
	- Would have to happen in the next 1-2 years for you to look back and think "this was the best hiring decision i ever made"
- **BACKGROUND**: Is there anything about my background that gives you a pause? 
- **LAST**: Is there anything you wished I should have asked you but didn't?



## Role / Nature of Work
- How much of the work is building new things vs implementing existing solutions?
- What's the most technically interesting problem someone in this role has solved recently?
- How much autonomy do I have to shape the technical approach?
- What does a typical week look like?

## Role Fit
- Based on everything you've heard, are there other roles you'd recommend I consider?
- Given my background (PhD, founder experience, production systems), which role do you think is the best fit?
- How common is it for people to move between teams internally?

## Team / People
- How big is the team? Who would I be working with day-to-day?
- What's the caliber of the team like? Where do people come from?
- How collaborative vs independent is the work?
- Who would I report to?

## Growth / Learning
- What does growth look like in this role? Where do people go from here?
- What's the biggest thing people learn in their first year?
- How much exposure to [specific area you want to learn] would I get?

## Process / Logistics
- What's the interview process from here?
- What's the timeline for making a decision?
- Is this role NYC-based? How does hybrid work?

## Company / Strategy
- What's the biggest bottleneck the team is trying to solve this year?
- What's changed recently in how you approach [X]?
- What's the company's biggest challenge right now?

## Closing
- Is there anything about my background that gives you pause?
- What else would be helpful for me to know?
- What are the next steps?

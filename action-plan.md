## Contact Strategy by Company Type

### Small Startups (Icarus, Viam, Radical AI, Standard Bots)
**Contact:** Founders / CTOs directly
**Why:** Founder-driven hiring, fast decisions

### Mid/Late Stage (Honeybee, Altana, Scale AI, Anduril, Palantir)
**Contact:** Hiring managers, team leads, senior engineers
**Avoid:** Celebrity CEOs (low response rate, gets forwarded to recruiting anyway)
**Why:** These have structured hiring; target people who control hiring for your role class

### AI Labs (DeepMind, FAIR, MSR, Hugging Face)
**Contact:** Team engineers first, then referrals
**Strategy:** Apply → Immediately follow with referral attempts
**Why:** Referrals matter more than portals. Team selection matters more than company name.

### Finance (Jane Street, DESRES)
**Contact:** PhD-specific recruiters
**Note:** DESRES is different from trading firms - scientific computing, closer to your background

### Don't Accept Early Offers Too Fast
If Icarus or Scale converts quickly but DeepMind/Applied Intuition are still in motion:
- Don't rush to yes in Week 2
- Negotiate start dates if needed

When time pressure hits, people default to startups (fast feedback, emotional resonance). Be conscious of that bias.

Protect time for:
- AI lab pipelines (started Week 1)
- Applied Intuition / NVIDIA (Week 2)

Even if startups respond faster.

### Finance Interview Prep
If Citadel/Jane Street replies → **STALL**
- You need 2+ weeks to prep for quant interviews

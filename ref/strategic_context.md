- ~2-3 month runway, small debt, no liquidity event yet
- Argues for prioritizing roles that rebuild cash buffer in 12-24 months

- Exiting (or semi-exiting) a founder chapter
- Deciding next identity: principal technologist, repeat founder, research leader, or capital accumulator
- Next role will disproportionately shape trajectory and self-concept
- No option will perfectly satisfy all objectives—choose moves that keep upside convex

### 3. Asymmetric Upside in Hard Domains
- Can credibly operate in controls, robotics, optimization, ML systems, AND startups simultaneously
- Creates asymmetric opportunity in: autonomy infrastructure, embodied AI, robotics platforms, simulation
- Drifting into generic software/AI product roles burns rare option value

### 4. NYC as Constraint and Filter
- Excellent AI product and finance ecosystem
- Thin robotics ecosystem, limited frontier AI research density
- Must be more precise in targeting; can't rely on density for serendipity
- But: lower competition density than SF for elite robotics roles
- **High-signal, low-volume market** for this niche

### 5. Timing Window
- Experienced enough for senior roles, young enough to pivot
- Still geographically mobile, still able to take some risk
- Family responsibilities will increase; tolerance for volatility may decline
- Window to make one more convex bet OR strongly capitalize position

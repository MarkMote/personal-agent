AI Pricing Co-Pilot for Franchise Businesses

What is your company going to make? Please describe your product and what it does or will do.
We’re building a platform that will help franchise businesses optimize their pricing strategy based on brand, location, and offering. 

Pricing is a complex problem that businesses struggle to solve correctly, resulting in lost employee-hours and less profit. Our platform addresses this by providing tailored recommendations on what the price should be, how it should be structured, how it should change over time, and what is happening with the market and competition. To do this, we collect data using custom AI agents (LLMs) to comb the web, and combine it with the information we collect from customers. This data powers a machine learning model that predicts optimal prices and forecasts trends. 

The platform is updated frequently for dynamic pricing. Initially, we’ll send monthly email reports to customers, but we plan to transition to an API and more interactive, dashboard interface along with a data marketplace.

Our MVP uses this information to auto-generate reports on recommended prices.

Who are your competitors? What do you understand about your business that they don't?
Most Common Solutions: 
Today, most private equity owned franchise businesses do pricing in-house or rely on pricing consultants. These approaches are inadequate due to their high-cost, manual nature, and inability to respond to new information. LLMs, combined with machine learning, are making it possible to do the job in a more effective and scalable way. 

Competition: 
Arena AI is the biggest, but mostly focused on consumer packaged goods and manufacturing. Accenture also provides prebuilt pricing models for various domains. 

What we Understand:

- Pricing must be considered in a holistic sense, with brand, promotions, time, area, and marketing closely intertwined.
- Every location is unique, but this unique value can be discovered through the data.  
- It’s all about the demand curve, competitor prices can only get you so far.

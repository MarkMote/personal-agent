What it is:
It's an online dashboard that provides tier-1 vehicle suppliers with forecasts on downstream sales and inventory levels. It uses language models to collect, structure, and analyze pricing and inventory information on the web. Using this data, it makes predictions on what will sell and be stocked by OEMs and dealerships over coming months. It also provides a range of graphs and tables to help customers understand whats happening in the supply chain today.

How it helps:
Suppliers need to plan inventory months in advance, but poor visibility into what will drive orders from other parts of the supply chain leads to over- or underproduction. The problem has been worse since COVID. Pytheia helps by providing the information suppliers need to match up what they stock with what customers will be willing to buy.

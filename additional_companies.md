# Additional Companies - Web Search Results (January 2026)

Research focus: Mid-stage (Series B+) NYC companies fitting robotics/aerospace/AI/controls profile.

## Tier 2 — Strong Fit (NYC Presence, Mid-Late Stage)

### Vannevar Labs
- **What:** Defense AI platform for national security. Agentic AI for DoD.
- **Stage:** Series B ($91M raised), $1.5B valuation (2025)
- **Location:** 66 Hudson Yards, NYC
- **Size:** Growing rapidly, profitable since 2022
- **Resume:** Chief Engineer / Product Founder
- **Why Interesting:** Defense AI with real traction ($80M run-rate revenue). Building agents that synthesize satellite/sensor/logistics data at scale. 1 PB of data growing 10 TB/week. Strong engineering culture.
- **Roles:** Senior ML Engineer (Agents), Forward Deployed Engineer, Senior Fullstack AI Engineer
- **Contact:** Via careers page
- **Source:** [Vannevar Labs Careers](https://www.vannevarlabs.com/careers/)

### Gecko Robotics
- **What:** AI-driven inspection robots for infrastructure (power plants, refineries, ships)
- **Stage:** Series D ($173M extended), $1.25B valuation
- **Location:** Pittsburgh HQ, **NYC office**, Boston, Houston, Austin, Europe
- **Size:** Growing
- **Resume:** Chief Engineer
- **Why Interesting:** Climbing/crawling/swimming robots with advanced sensors. Cantilever AI platform. Real industrial deployment. Defense and energy customers.
- **Roles:** Robotics engineers, AI/ML engineers
- **Contact:** Via careers page
- **Source:** [Gecko Robotics](https://www.geckorobotics.com/)

### Hyperscience
- **What:** AI automation for document processing and back-office workflows
- **Stage:** Growth (Bessemer, Battery, Tiger Global backed)
- **Location:** NYC HQ
- **Size:** Mid-stage
- **Resume:** Chief Engineer / Distinguished Academic
- **Why Interesting:** Leader in Gartner Magic Quadrant for IDP. Enterprise AI at scale. ML engineering challenges with unstructured data.
- **Roles:** ML engineers, Technical Delivery Partners
- **Contact:** Via careers page
- **Source:** [Hyperscience Careers](https://www.hyperscience.ai/about-us/careers/)

### Dataiku
- **What:** Universal AI Platform for enterprise ML/analytics
- **Stage:** Series F ($200M), $4.6B valuation
- **Location:** NYC office (also Paris HQ, Denver, DC, LA)
- **Size:** 1,100+ employees, 700+ enterprise customers
- **Resume:** Product Founder / Chief Engineer
- **Why Interesting:** Major AI platform company. Engineering teams work on Spark, Kubernetes, deep learning. Less robotics-focused but strong ML infrastructure.
- **Roles:** Software engineers, ML engineers, presales
- **Contact:** Via careers page
- **Source:** [Dataiku Careers](https://www.dataiku.com/careers/)

### Hebbia
- **What:** AI platform for finance (RAG, document analysis for asset managers)
- **Stage:** Series B ($161M raised), a16z/Peter Thiel backed
- **Location:** 233 Spring Street, SoHo NYC
- **Size:** Scaling fast (2x headcount planned for 2025)
- **Resume:** Distinguished Academic / Product Founder
- **Why Interesting:** AI for finance vertical. Powers BlackRock, KKR, Carlyle. Proprietary ISD architecture. Less robotics but interesting AI infrastructure challenges.
- **Roles:** Backend engineers, platform engineers, distributed systems, data engineers
- **Contact:** Via careers page
- **Source:** [Hebbia Careers](https://careers.hebbia.ai/)

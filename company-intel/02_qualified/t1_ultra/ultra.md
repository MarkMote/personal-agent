# Ultra

## Quick Facts
- **Location:** NYC (in-person)
- **Stage:** Seed / YC W24 (4 employees)
- **Founded:** 2024
- **Focus:** General-purpose robots for repetitive industrial tasks

## The Problem They're Solving
Traditional industrial automation is rigid and complex, requiring weeks of integration. Ultra builds practical, general-purpose robots that are zero-integration (installed in hours) and highly flexible (quickly learn new tasks).

## Their Approach
- Robots automate e-commerce order packaging and returns in fulfillment centers
- Zero integration - can be deployed in hours, not weeks
- Flexible - quickly learns new tasks
- Already have robots in the field generating revenue and data

## Tech Stack / Skills Needed
- IK solvers and low-level control loops
- Neural network control interfaces
- Low-latency systems (video encoding, networking, middleware)
- Hardware integration and driver development
- Teleoperation systems

# Viam

## Quick Facts
- **Location:** NYC HQ (hybrid 3+ days/week)
- **Stage:** Series B+ (51-200 employees)
- **Founded:** 2020 by Eliot Horowitz (MongoDB co-founder/CTO)
- **Focus:** Robotics OS platform - configure and control physical devices at scale

## The Problem They're Solving
Robotics software is fragmented and hard to scale. Every team reinvents the wheel on device management, data collection, and control. Viam provides a unified platform so engineers can focus on their application, not infrastructure.

## Their Approach
- Full-service engineering platform for physical devices (robots, sensors, cameras, IoT)
- Cloud-based configuration, monitoring, and control
- Works across industries: food & bev, climate tech, marine, industrial manufacturing
- SDKs and APIs for integration

## Tech Stack
- Backend: Go, MongoDB, GCP
- Frontend: Svelte, Vue
- Edge: Linux on ARM devices
- Motion team uses Go, C++

# Standard Bots

## Quick Facts
- **Location:** Glen Cove, NY (HQ) + NYC office (Grand Central)
- **Stage:** Series B ($63M) - General Catalyst, Amazon Industrial, Samsung Next
- **Focus:** Accessible robot arms (RO1/RO3), AI-powered automation

## The Problem They're Solving
Industrial automation is inaccessible - requires specialized robotics expertise. Standard Bots makes robot arms that anyone can program, "bringing the power of bits to the world of atoms."

## Their Approach
- Hardware + software together for accessibility
- "No robotics background required" philosophy
- Teach robots by hand (simple programming model)
- Starting with manufacturers, vision to expand to every home and business

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React, TypeScript
- Backend: Node.js, Python, C++
- Motion: ROS2, MoveIt2
- Infrastructure: Kubernetes, Docker, GitHub Actions

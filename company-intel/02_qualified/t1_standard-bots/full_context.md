Senior Software Engineer, Motion Planning

About Standard Bots

Standard Bot's mission is to significantly lower the barrier to entry to real-world automation, bringing the power of bits to the world of atoms and unlocking productivity for entire new industries and users. We specialize in developing intuitive solutions for robotic arm manipulation and control, making advanced motion planning accessible to a broader range of industries.

Our user facing applications and APIs directly empower individuals and developers to jump directly into solving their challenges through sophisticated yet approachable robotic arm control systems. A relentless focus on simplicity and ease of use brings this revolutionary potential to new users and industries.

What you'll do

We're looking for seasoned and skilled technical leaders to help architect the future of robotics through best in class, modern software engineering practices. As a Senior Software Engineer on our motion planning team, you will work with other engineers, organizational leaders, and users to advance our robotic arm control systems and unlock entirely new potential for our users.

Responsibilities

    Implement core functionality surrounding our motion planning service

    Identify, scope, and drive high impact technical projects in robotic manipulation

    Advise leadership on technical decisions and strategy in robotics

    Mentor and advise other engineers on best practices and motion planning concepts

Tech Stack

    Stack: Node.js, Python, C++

    Motion Planning: ROS2, MoveIt2

    Infrastructure: Docker, Github Actions

Who you are

We're looking for engineers who are self-driven, optimistic, creative, and resourceful who are interested in working deep in a robotics stack. Motion planning experience is required for this role.

Skills you'll bring

    Motion planning experience: You have experience writing motion planning algorithms. Robotic arm experience is preferred, but motion planning for autonomous vehicles and related domains is also considered.

    Backend experience: You have covered all parts of driving a project to completion, focusing mainly on backend services

    Experience: You have a strong technical background, including 5+ total years of experience in software engineering, with ideally 3+ of those years working with robotic systems and complex software environments. In lieu of formal work experience, we will also consider a relevant PhD + research projects.

    Growth + Optimism: You are excited by challenging robotics problems and eager to make sophisticated motion planning accessible to non-experts

    Clarity of Communication: You excel in written and verbal communication and can effectively explain complex robotics concepts to diverse audiences

    Impact Driven: You relentlessly seek user impact and value practical solutions over technical idealism

    Proactive Simplicity: You prioritize simplicity, reliability, and velocity in robotics applications and proactively identify opportunities to improve motion planning systems

    Excelling in Ambitious Environments: You excel in dynamic and ambitious environments, working with a diverse group of individuals and backgrounds

Nice to Haves

    Algorithmic focused experience: Many of our projects require diving deeper into the motion planning algorithm stack, so an algorithmic focused background is preferred

    ROS/Moveit2 Experience: We prefer someone with ROS experience to quickly get up to speed with our motion planning stack

    Full-stack Experience: While this role is primarily backend, familiarity with frontend development is valuable in our full-stack environment.

Compensation and Benefits:

The salary range for this role is $180,000 to $240,000, depending on experience. We are open to a variety of seniority levels for this role and will build compensation packages that are commensurate with seniority and skill level. Base salary is just one part of the overall compensation at Standard Bots. All Full-Time Employees are eligible for Employee Stock Options. We also offer a package of benefits including paid time off, medical/dental/vision insurance, life insurance, disability insurance, and 401(k) to regular full-time employees.

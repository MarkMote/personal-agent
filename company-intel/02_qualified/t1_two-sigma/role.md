## Role

ML/AI Engineer bridging innovative research with production-level deployment. Work with researchers, data teams, and platform engineers to transform ideas into scalable, robust production systems. Drive best practices across the ML lifecycle from prototyping to inference.

**Responsibilities:**
- Collaborate with ML researchers on model architectures, training methodologies, experimental objectives
- Translate prototypes into production-ready code
- Design and optimize automated training pipelines and evaluation workflows
- Optimize models for production (quantization, distillation, intelligent batching)
- Assess and fine-tune research and production workflows
- Identify process improvements to reduce bottlenecks
- Stay ahead of industry trends, incorporate new methodologies
- Provide technical guidance and mentorship to peers

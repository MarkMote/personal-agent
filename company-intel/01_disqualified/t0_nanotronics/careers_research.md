## Best Fit Role

**Machine Learning Engineer, AI Lab** or **Software Architect, nControl** are the strongest matches because:

1. **Controls + Physics emphasis** - JD explicitly mentions "grasp high order concepts in physics, control theory" which maps directly to PhD background
2. **AI/ML platform work** - building ML lifecycle systems aligns with optimization/systems thinking
3. **Hardware-software integration** - microscopy + AI is the kind of deep tech that benefits from research rigor
4. **Brooklyn location** - on-site at BNY, fits NYC constraint

If those specific roles aren't active, the **General Applications** route is viable given:
- Company is small (~100-200 people based on job creation targets)
- They emphasize "early-career potential can sometimes be as important as senior-level experience"
- Direct outreach to Matthew Putman or engineering leadership may surface unlisted needs

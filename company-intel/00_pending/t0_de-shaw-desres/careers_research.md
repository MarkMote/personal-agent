**Research Date:** January 16, 2026
**Source:** [deshawresearch.com/current-opportunities.html](https://www.deshawresearch.com/current-opportunities.html)

## Company Overview

D.E. Shaw Research (DESRES) is a computational research organization separate from D.E. Shaw's quant finance arm. They develop and use advanced computational technologies to understand biomolecular behavior at atomic detail and design targeted drugs. Core technology is **Anton**, a proprietary special-purpose supercomputer for molecular dynamics simulation.

**Location:** New York, NY (all roles are NYC-based, hybrid Tue-Thu in office)
**Size:** ~161 employees
**Culture:** Strong scientific/academic feel, 86% employee recommendation rate on Glassdoor

### Hivemind (AI Pilot)
The core product - an AI pilot that can operate in GPS-denied, comms-denied environments. Three pillars:
- **Hivemind Edge** - Autonomy software running on the vehicle (perception, planning, action)
- **Hivemind Design** - Dev tools for capability development and testing
- **Hivemind Commander** - Human-machine interfaces for mission planning and C2

**Key capability:** Swarming - multiple UAS commanded by minimal operators, scaling combat power

# D.E. Shaw — Applied AI Engineer

**Source:** [deshaw.com/careers/applied-ai-engineer-5375](https://www.deshaw.com/careers/applied-ai-engineer-5375)
**Also see:** [Glassdoor listing](https://www.glassdoor.com/job-listing/software-developer-generative-ai-d-e-shaw-and-co-investment-firm-JV_KO0,32_KE33,64.htm?jl=1009615110041)

## Overview

The D. E. Shaw group seeks exceptional software engineers with expertise in applied AI, AI agents, and agentic systems. The role offers the chance to work directly with a variety of groups at the firm on innovative, greenfield projects that transform how teams operate, leveraging quantitative and programming skills to design, build, and deploy AI solutions that drive efficiency, enhance analytical capabilities, and accelerate decision-making across the firm.

## Responsibilities

- Collaborate directly with internal groups and end users across various functions to build bespoke AI agents and applications tailored to nuanced, real-world business needs.
- Lead and contribute to greenfield AI projects, taking ownership from concept through production and helping shape internal AI strategy and adoption.
- Experiment with emerging AI tools and model capabilities, rapidly prototyping and integrating them across platforms to enhance usability and scalability.
- Build foundational AI components: agent frameworks, reusable "skills," and large-scale retrieval systems to support AI tools and applications.
- Design, develop, and maintain shared AI infrastructure and agentic applications, ensuring firmwide data integration and enhancing software development efficiency.

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in any field
- Extensive background in software development
- Hands-on experience building and scaling AI solutions at the product, system, or company level
- Solid understanding of AI technologies and interest in developing advanced AI applications and frameworks
- Demonstrated ability to thrive in technical or entrepreneurial environments
- Capability to solve complex challenges and lead projects from inception to deployment

## Compensation

- Base: $200,000 - $250,000
- Year-end bonus (guaranteed in first year)
- Benefits: medical, 401(k) matching, wellness reimbursement, family building, charitable gift match

## How This Maps to Mark

- "Build bespoke AI agents" = exactly Roostr (rate ingestor, quoting agent, Slackbot)
- "Greenfield projects, concept through production" = both startups, zero-to-one
- "Agent frameworks, reusable skills, large-scale retrieval" = RAG, tool use, agentic orchestration
- "Thrive in entrepreneurial environments" = two-time founder

### Questions for Them

General: 
- **ROLE**: How open-ended is the role? Is it mainly building new things or implementing existing solutions? 
	- What kinds of things are built
	- Which role is best?  
- **TEAM**: Can you tell me about the team I'd be working with? Whats the size and typical background. 
- **PROBLEMS**: whats the biggest problem or bottleneck the team is trying to solve right now? 
- **GROWTH**: What does growth look like in this role? Where do people go from here in 5 or 10 years? 
- **SUCCESS**: What does it take to be successful here? 
	- Would have to happen in the next 1-2 years for you to look back and think "this was the best hiring decision i ever made"
- **BACKGROUND**: Is there anything about my background that gives you a pause? 
- **SUPRISE**: What's suprised you most about this company since joining? 
- **LAST**: Is there anything you wished I should have asked you but didn't?

## The Problem They're Solving
Traditional industrial automation is rigid and complex, requiring weeks of integration. Ultra builds practical, general-purpose robots that are zero-integration (installed in hours) and highly flexible (quickly learn new tasks via neural net control trained from demonstrations).

## Their Approach
- Robots automate e-commerce order packaging and returns in fulfillment centers
- Currently packing poly bags and padded mailers; box packing launching next
- Neural net controllers trained from examples (teleoperation), not explicitly programmed
- Zero integration: deployed in 1-2 days, no floor bolts or construction
- Service model: $2,500-$3,000/month per robot
- Already deployed at customer sites, generating revenue

## Traction (as of Dec 2025 podcast)

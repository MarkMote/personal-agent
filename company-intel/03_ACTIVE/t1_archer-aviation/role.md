## About Archer
Aerospace company building all-electric VTOL aircraft. Mission: advance sustainable air mobility. Designing, manufacturing, and operating aircraft carrying 4 passengers with minimal noise.

---

## What You'll Do

- **Architect Autonomy Frameworks:** Lead system architecture and feature development for autonomous flight systems - multi-agent coordination, task allocation, resource scheduling in real-time
- **GNC Software Integration:** Design, implement, integrate C++ product software for guidance, navigation, and control. Ensure portability and scalability across platforms, oversee transition from algorithmic design to embedded deployment
- **State Estimation & Localization:** SME for state estimation and localization. Develop software fusing passive sensing, GPS, IMU for robust navigation (day, night, off-road)
- **Cross-Functional Leadership:** Partner with Systems Engineering and Flight Test. Lead definition of verifiable software requirements, integrate GNC into vehicle navigation and obstacle avoidance
- **Safety & Compliance:** Direct development of safety-critical autonomy software. Coordinate safety compliance with FAA/NASA. Manage rigorous validation for flight control and autonomous path following
- **Simulation Infrastructure:** Champion simulation and HITL test infrastructure. Drive performance analysis of compute loads and communications
- **Mentorship & Methodology:** Lead engineering teams in Agile environments. Mentor engineers in autonomy and robotics best practices

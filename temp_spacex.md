**Location:** Starbase, TX
**Discipline:** Engineering - Aerospace & Mechanical
**Pay:** Not listed

### Overview
SpaceX seeks a senior guidance, navigation, and control engineer to develop flight control systems for Starship — "a fully reusable spacecraft capable of carrying humans to Mars and beyond."

### Responsibilities
- Develop guidance, navigation, and control systems for Starship
- Conduct vehicle systems investigations and design work
- Implement software and algorithms for mission-specific capabilities
- Create 6-DOF dynamics simulation models
- Analyze trajectories, performance dispersions, and flight stability
- Review post-flight data and correlate with pre-flight analyses
- Support launch and mission operations
- Perform comprehensive GNC software testing including hardware-in-the-loop and Monte Carlo analyses

### Basic Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in physics or engineering
- 5+ years professional experience
- Expertise in control systems, orbital mechanics, dynamics, modeling, and simulation
- Proficiency in Python or C++

### Preferred Qualifications
- Postgraduate degree (Master's or PhD)
- Aerodynamics knowledge
- Sensor/actuator expertise
- Launch vehicle experience
- Independent problem-solving ability
- Passion for commercial spaceflight
- Strong communication skills

### Additional Requirements
- Must accept extended hours and/or weekends as needed
- ITAR: U.S. citizens, permanent residents, refugees, asylees, or eligible for State Dept authorization
